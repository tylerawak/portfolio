Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TylerAWak collects, uses, and stores information across the following websites:

  • tylerawak.net — Portfolio
  • ytclip.tylerawak.net — YTClip
  • meetgrid.tylerawak.net — MeetGrid
  • sn2.tylerawak.net — SN2 Blueprint Tracker

These sites are free tools. No account is required to use any of them.

Information We Collect

All sites — Bot protection cookie

All four sites use Cloudflare Turnstile to verify that visitors are human. When you pass the verification check, a cookie named tw_ok is set in your browser. This cookie contains only a cryptographic timestamp — no personal information. It expires after 24 hours. This check typically completes automatically in the background without any interaction required.

MeetGrid — Event data

When you create a scheduling event on MeetGrid, the following information is stored on our servers:

  • The event name you choose
  • The dates and time range you select
  • Participant availability responses (which time slots are marked available/tentative/unavailable)
  • The participant name you enter, if any (this field is optional and can be left blank)

This data is stored in a Cloudflare D1 database and is automatically and permanently deleted 30 days after the event was created. We do not link this data to any individual identity.

SN2 Blueprint Tracker — Local storage

The SN2 Blueprint Tracker saves your build queue, inventory, and crafted items in your browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is never sent to any server. Clearing your browser data will erase it.

YTClip — No data stored

YTClip processes YouTube video IDs and timestamps entirely in your browser using URL parameters. No data is stored on our servers. The clip links you generate are self-contained URLs.

Portfolio — No data stored

The portfolio site does not collect or store any personal information.

Advertising — Google AdSense

These sites display advertisements served by Google AdSense. Google uses cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to these sites and/or other sites on the Internet.

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings. You can also opt out through the Network Advertising Initiative opt-out page.

For more information on how Google uses data when you use our sites, visit: How Google uses data from sites that use Google services.

Third-Party Services

These sites interact with the following third-party services:

  • Cloudflare — Provides hosting, CDN, and the Turnstile bot-protection widget. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • YouTube / Google — YTClip embeds the YouTube IFrame Player API to load videos in your browser. Google Privacy Policy
  • Google AdSense — Displays advertisements. Google Privacy Policy

Cookies

The following cookies may be set when you visit these sites:

  • tw_ok — Set by our Turnstile bot-protection check. Contains a signed timestamp only. Expires after 24 hours.
  • Google AdSense cookies — Set by Google for ad personalization and frequency capping. See Google's cookie policy for details.

No analytics cookies (e.g. Google Analytics) are used on these sites.

Data Retention

  • MeetGrid events and responses: deleted automatically 30 days after creation
  • tw_ok cookie: expires after 24 hours
  • SN2 local data: stored in your browser only, until you clear it
  • All other data: not stored

Children's Privacy

These sites are not directed at children under 13 and do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a revised effective date.
